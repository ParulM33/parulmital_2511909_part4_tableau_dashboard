# Chart Selection Justification
    
    ## Sales Trend Chart
     
    A line chart is used to show changes in sales over time.  
  It helps identify trends and seasonal patterns.
    
    ## Regional Performance
   
   A bar chart is used to compare performance across regions.  
    This allows easy comparison of sales and profit.
    
     ## Category Profitability
     
     A bar chart or treemap is used to compare category contribution.  
     It highlights top and low performing categories.
     
    ## Discount vs Profit
     A scatter plot is used to show relationship between discount and profit.  
     This helps in understanding impact of discount on margins.
    
     ## Shipping Performance
     
     A bar chart is used to compare shipping modes.  
    It highlights delays and efficiency differences.
